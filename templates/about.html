About Us

Welcome to our web application! Our goal is to connect people traveling in the same direction so they can share rides and reduce fuel costs and the number of cars on the road.

We believe carpooling is a sustainable solution for our planet, and we're building a community to make it happen.

If you have any questions or ideas, feel free to reach out to us!
